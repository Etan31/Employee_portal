# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Employees at a company (Philippines-based, desk and field workers) managing their own leave requests, tracking attendance, and reviewing work-hour analytics. Primary task on any screen: submit a leave request, check a remaining balance, or scan attendance history.

## Product Purpose
Nexus Employee Portal — internal HR self-service tool. Employees submit and track leave requests, view attendance and work-hour history, check leave balances, and see upcoming public holidays. Success: any HR self-service task completed in under two minutes without calling HR.

## Brand Personality
Professional, clear, trustworthy. The product disappears into the task. Not corporate-cold, not playful — efficient with warmth.

## Anti-references
- Legacy HRIS clutter (SAP, Oracle HCM): dense tables, tiny text, confusing multi-step flows
- Over-polished SaaS dashboards with decorative motion and hero-metric layouts — this is a tool, not a marketing page
- Consumer mobile apps with oversized rounded cards and excessive whitespace

## Design Principles
1. **Task first** — every layout decision serves the primary task; decoration is a tax
2. **Data is honest** — charts and metrics reflect real values with no vanity numbers
3. **Familiar affordances** — standard controls, standard nav; no invented patterns
4. **Density without crowding** — information-rich but scannable
5. **Accessible by default** — WCAG AA minimum; employees at all roles use this

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA minimum. Reduced motion always honored. Color is never the only signal — status badges carry both color and text label.
